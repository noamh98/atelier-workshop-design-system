ATELIER · Workshop DS
Enterprise Presentation Design System

The strategy workshop, rendered like a consultant's notebook.

A reusable component framework for executive decks — AI, digital transformation, SAP, Power BI, government, insurance, banking, healthcare & cyber. Paper texture, ink system, hand-drawn annotations. Built with HTML · CSS · Vanilla JS. No frameworks.

01 · FOUNDATION

Ink System

Every mark on the page carries meaning. Six semantic inks drive the whole system.

Black — Titles

Structure, headings, the skeleton of the argument.

Blue — Ideas

Hypotheses, concepts, links and connective thinking.

Red — Problems

Pain points, blockers, what is broken today.

Green — Solutions

Recommendations, wins, the desired future state.

Orange — Risks

Uncertainty, exposure, things to watch closely.

Purple — AI

Intelligence, automation, model-driven capability.
